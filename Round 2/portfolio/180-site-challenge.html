This is the 180 websites challenge.

I am going to be doing something similar to Jennifer Dewalt's 180 sites in 180 days. I will be building 180 sites but I will not be limiting myself to the 180 days. I will allow myself to work on more than one site a day or use multiple days to complete a more complicated site.

My goals for this challenge are:

  • Code everyday.
  • Slowly ramp up the difficulty.
  • Build upon my existing knowledge.
  • Try new things and not be afraid to make mistakes.
  • Become a better programmer with cleaner DRY(Don't Repeat Yourself) code.
  • Not to worry about how pretty each project is.

I want to make this challenge fun and light-hearted so I will be looking for ways to add a personal flair to the projects. I will be keeping track of all my progress on this site, Github, and Codepen.
